Motivation.

By conducting this first study, we intend to:

- Realize which color mixtures are more easily perceived by the user.

- Realize if the mixture of colors is an appropriate channel to convey information.

- Conclude if the user is capable of, not only building a mental color mixture model, but also deconstructing mixtures into their basic components.
