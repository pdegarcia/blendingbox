Instructions.

Please, follow the steps below indicated and answer the questions.

(Step 1) - If possible, adjust your room lights for a normal vision condition.

(Step 2) - Avoid reflections on your screen, by diverting the screen from direct sources of light. This step is important, since light reflections can affect visualization of images.

(Step 3) - To adjust the Black Point of your screen, define the Contrast and Brightness of your screen to their maximum..

(Step 4) - After Step 3, gradually reduce Brightness value of your screen, in order to correctly distinguish the squares of each image below.

(Step 5) - If possible, define the Color Temperature of your screen to 6500 Kelvin Degrees.

(Step 6) - You can start the Calibration Phase.
