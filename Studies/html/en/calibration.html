Step Two - Screen Calibration.

Let's Calibrate Your Screen.

In this step, it's going to be presented to you a set of images. You should tune you screen definitions, in order to answer the questions, keeping them until the end of this study.

Instructions.

Please, follow the steps below indicated and answer the questions.

(Step 1) - If possible, adjust your room lights for a comfortable usage of your device.

(Step 2) - Avoid reflections on your screen, by diverting the screen from direct sources of light. This step is important, since light reflections can affect visualization of images.

(Step 3) - To adjust the Black Point of your screen, define the Contrast and Brightness of your screen to their maximum..

(Step 4) - After Step 3, gradually reduce Brightness value of your screen, in order to correctly distinguish the squares of each image below.

(Step 5) - If possible, define the Color Temperature of your screen to 6500 Kelvin Degrees.

(Step 6) - You are now ready to answer the following questions!

Question 1 of 2.

In the following image, there are presented different squares with different shades of black, numbered in red. Which is the last perceivable square for you? Write in the according textboxes below the image, the word and the correspondent number of that square, or NONE if you cannot detect any word or square.

Question 2 of 2.

In this image, there are presented different squares with different shades of white, numbered in red. Which is the last perceivable square for you? Write in the according textboxes below the image, the word and the correspondent number of that square, or NONE if you cannot detect any word or square.
